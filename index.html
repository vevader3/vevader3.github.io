Problem Statement: College Navigation App

In the University of South Carolina, there is no one good way to figure out where to get to your classes as an incoming student. While a map of the buildings is provided, students are left clueless as to where in the massive buildings their classrooms are. Our app aims to provide an interactive and simple way to help students better locate their classes and navigate through campus.

Affinity Diagram

My Group and I worked together to brainstorm any possible limitations of our idea, and to create solutions to these potential problems. As a result, I believe that our app now will work better for its users.

This is a sketch that I made as a demonstration of what our app could actually look like to its users. Users would input what building and classroom they want to go to, and the app will give them directions both to the building and the room itself.
